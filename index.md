## Current role

**Senior AVP — AI Transformation, HSBC** · Jan 2022 – Present · Bengaluru

Leading AI transformation across HSBC IWPB, delivering production-grade agentic systems, transformer-based architectures, and LLM-powered platforms.

**Selected highlights:**

- **Autonomous Fraud Intelligence Platform** — closed-loop agentic system (Monitor → Feature Discovery → Retrain) that autonomously detects model drift and rebuilds challenger models. Reduced model redevelopment cycle by 68%.
- **Enterprise Multi-Agent KYC Onboarding Platform** — end-to-end multi-market KYC automation built in partnership with Microsoft as a reference architecture. Reduced manual review effort by 84%, ~$1.2M annual savings.
- **Next Best Action Intelligence Engine** — knowledge graph (TransE embeddings) fused with a transformer encoder via cross-attention, validated on 2M+ customer interactions with a 16% Recall@3 improvement over baseline.
- **Enterprise Knowledge Intelligence Platform** — production-grade RAG (multi-query, hybrid retrieval, reranking) with LLMOps instrumentation and AI safety guardrails, deployed on Azure.
